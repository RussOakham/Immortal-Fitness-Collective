About Us!

Immortal Fitness Collective

Anchored by our physical gym, located in Bristol, United Kingdom, International Fitness Collective is the international brand behind a number of top performing CrossFit athletes.

Our team prides itself on providing the highest quality apparel to athletes of all levels, built around our three design pillars of being comfortable, effective and durable.

Family, Fitness, Service is more than a motto, it is our compass and what propels us forward.

Meet the Team

Dan Mallet CEO & Head Coach

Dan specializes in strength and conditioning. He is the programming expert for the community and has helped thousands of members achieve things they never though possible.
Dan's favourite movement is the clean and favourite CrossFit benchmark workout is Cindy.

Noah Hammer Coach & Head Nutritionist

Noah joined the IFC team in June 2020, joining with plenty of energy and years of programming experience. Noah is helping Dan with our current programming, plus working on our new nutrition programme coming soon!
Noah's favourite movement is the snatch and favourite CrossFit benchmark is Murph.

Charlotte Tong Head of Apparel

Charlotte is a long time member of the IFC team. As Head of Apparel, she is responsible for managing the design and delivery of all our merchandise. Outside of her merch responsibilities, Charlotte is also a keen endurance athlete and is currently training for a half Ironman.
Charlotte's favourite movement is the muscle up and favourite CrossFit benchmark workout is Kelly.
